Spice the chicken with onion, thyme, curry and salt.
Step 2

Step 3
Fry tomato paste, pepper and onion add water and let it boil then add curry, thyme, jollof rice spicy, chicken stock pour in Parboiled rice stir very well.

Cover and boil until is well done
